MYA‑GEN’s property stem directly addresses these issues by:
Digitizing and decentralizing property records, making them tamper-proof and accessible.

Integrating with legal frameworks for enforceability and dispute resolution.
Empirical studies show that when transaction costs are reduced alongside property rights reforms, economic outcomes (income, credit, innovation) improve significantly, while unemployment and misallocation decline.

Embedding AI-powered threat detection and response.
Enforcing zero-trust and multifactor authentication across all layers.
Integrating cyber and physical incident response, with continuous monitoring and adaptive governance.
